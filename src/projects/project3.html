THE BOX

a life changing product

2019

The box is a product like never before. You can teleport, get instant delivery and even fly! What more could you want? It uses quantum entanglement to achieve the impossible.

This was one of the most challenging projects to create because it involved eveything we have learned throughout the whole year. Putting it all together was hardest part and took the most time, because of the large amount of footage. I am very happy with the work but I also believe that it could have been better if we managed our time more effectively. For example we finished editing the video, the day it was due. So we did not have the time to blend all of the parts together. I've learned that time managment is the most important in any project.
