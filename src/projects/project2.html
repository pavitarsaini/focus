THE PATH

animation | low poly

a journey of two friends

2019

There is always more then one path and choosing one and sticking with it is easy is easy if you make the best out of it.

I created this animation to show that there is never a wrong path. One may be harder than the other but both lead to success in the end. This Low poly animation took lots of work but I was very satisfied with the look and feel of it. I've always enjoyed low poly design because its simple and makes objects pop out.
